About

I’m Junhewk Kim, a pediatric dentist and writer/translator. I’m studying medical humanities and ethics.

My personal theme is:

  • Narrative study (with literary critics and digital humanities) related to medicine
  • Medical ethics (especially narrative ethics and influence of information technology)

I’m studying at the department of medical ethics and health policy, University of Pennsylvania. If you have anything to ask about the text analysis or medical ethics, mail to me (junhewk.kim@gmail.com).
